## Title Card Prompt (16:9)

Create a professional title card for a software tool called "SynapseWatcher".

Visual style: Alert, vigilant, monitoring aesthetic with tech elements. Use a dark background (#0a0a1a) with electric blue (#00d4ff), gold (#ffd700), and green accents.

Main elements:
- Bold title "SynapseWatcher" in modern sans-serif font (left-center)
- Tagline: "Real-Time Message Notifications" in smaller text below title
- Right side: Large stylized eye or radar dish radiating detection waves
- Concentric circles or waves emanating outward (signal detection)
- Small notification bell or alert symbols integrated into design
- Subtle geometric pattern suggesting constant monitoring (24/7)
- Glowing pulse effect suggesting "always on" state

Layout: Horizontal 16:9 format, title on left third, monitoring visuals on right two-thirds.

Style: Professional, alert, vigilant. Think "security camera meets notification system meets radar". Emphasis on instant detection and constant monitoring.

## App Icon Prompt (1:1)

Create a square app icon for "SynapseWatcher".

Visual style: Bold, instantly recognizable monitoring symbol. Electric blue and gold on dark background.

Main elements:
- Central stylized eye OR radar dish (choose one, keep it simple)
- Single radiating wave or pulse ring
- Small notification dot in corner
- Minimal, high contrast
- Looks great at 256x256 and 64x64

Layout: Square 1:1, centered icon, very simple and bold.

Style: Instantly recognizable as a watching/monitoring tool. Think "notification meets surveillance meets alert system".
